What is our goal here?

a.k.a. demystifying learning outcomes

Learning outcomes

1

Analyse & apply key statistical concepts:

  • probability distributions
  • parameters, estimators & sampling distributions
  • point & interval estimation
  • hypothesis testing

What does this mean in practice?

You should understand the building blocks of statistics as applied to economic questions:

  • how we use samples to learn about a bigger group
  • how confident we can be in our estimates
  • how to tell if a result is real or just random

Learning outcomes

2

Specify, estimate & interpret a regression model

What does this mean in practice?

You should be able to set up and use regression models to
examine data & models

  • explore how one variable (education) affects another (income)
  • interpret regression tables (how economists present results)

Learning outcomes

2

Specify, estimate & interpret a regression model

Answer: Did it work?

Learning outcomes

3

Summarise and interpret the estimation results, and draw valid inferences using hypothesis tests

What does this mean in practice?

Make sense of your results! Basically, you should know…

  • what the numbers mean
  • how strong the evidence is
  • whether your conclusions are “real” or could be due to chance

Move from output \rightarrow insight

Learning outcomes

4

Critically evaluate standard regression model assumptions & the consequences of violations

What does this mean in practice?

You should know and understand…

  • what can go wrong when the assumptions underlying a regression model don’t hold up
  • what such violations mean for your results

Become informed consumers

Learning outcomes

5

Use econometric software to solve econometric problems

What does this mean in practice?

Use software to run econometric analyses

Build practical skills!

Assessment

Quiz: 20%

Checks your grasp of core concepts and skills in applied econometrics, building on work from tutorials

Format: online quiz

Problem set: 30%

You’ll draw on content from lectures & tutorials to apply econometrics to real problems
