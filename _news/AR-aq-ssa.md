:tada: My review "[Air Quality in Sub-Saharan Africa: Measurement Issues and Empirical Challenges](https://emiliatjernstrom.com/assets/pdf/Tjernstrom_2026.pdf)" is forthcoming in the Annual Review of Resource Economics! Sub-Saharan Africa hosts under 2% of the world's reference-grade air quality monitors, so most pollution estimates come from satellites and models---and the resulting measurement error is anything but classical. The review maps how these data products work, where their errors come from, and what that means for empirical research designs.
